Fit & Fab Annual Report 2023

A Comprehensive Data Analytics Portfolio Project

Business Impact

This dashboard helps businesses identify top-performing regions, understand customer behavior, and optimize sales channels. It enables data-driven decision-making to improve sales performance, reduce order cancellations, and enhance customer targeting.

Interactive Dashboard

Key Insights

Regional Leaders

Maharashtra and Karnataka consistently generate the highest sales volume, serving as the primary geographic drivers for revenue.

Demographic Dominance

Women contribute significantly more to total sales compared to men, indicating a strong product-market fit in the female apparel category.

Top Channel Performance

Myntra emerges as the absolute top-performing sales channel, vastly outperforming other e-commerce platforms like Amazon and Flipkart.

Fulfillment Success

The vast majority of orders are successfully delivered (over 92%), maintaining an exceptionally low cancellation and return rate.

Seasonal Trends

Sales exhibit consistent momentum throughout the year, with distinct volume peaks occurring in key purchasing months.

About The Project

The Fit & Fab Annual Report 2023 was developed to transform raw sales data into actionable business intelligence. By leveraging advanced data visualization techniques, this project provides a comprehensive overview of e-commerce performance across multiple dimensions.

Detailed Business Impact

In today's competitive e-commerce landscape, relying on intuition is no longer sufficient. This dashboard provides a strategic advantage by allowing stakeholders to:

  • Optimize Inventory Distribution: By identifying Maharashtra and Karnataka as top states, supply chain logistics can be prioritized to reduce shipping times.
  • Refine Marketing Spend: Knowing that Myntra is the dominant channel allows for targeted ad allocations, maximizing Return on Ad Spend (ROAS).
  • Tailor Product Offerings: Recognizing the strong sales contribution from women helps in curating future collections and promotional campaigns.
